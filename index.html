Krushnachandra Panigrahy

Department of Computational and Data Sciences
Indian Institute of Science
Bangalore - 560 012

I currently hold the position of a project associate in the Department of Computational and Data Sciences (CDS) at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) in Bangalore. My current engagement involves active participation in the Numerical Algorithm and Tensor Learning (NATL) Lab at CDS, IISc.

Before joining this role, I served as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Engineering Mathematics at K L University, located in Hyderabad, Telangana. In 2021, I earned my doctoral degree from the National Institute of Technology Raipur in Chhattisgarh.
